About Grand Master
Affan Kutty

From the busy lanes of Mumbra, Thane, Affan Kutty turned his passion for the classic cube into a spectacular journey of performance-art, records and inspiration. He isn’t just a solver – he’s a creator, an entertainer and a mentor.

Having completed over 1,000 cube-mosaic artworks, and honoured with titles such as “Grand Master” for his innovation in the cube-art space, his work spans from elite stage shows to creative installations in schools, events and corporate functions.

Affan’s mission: to transform what many see as a toy into a tool for awe, learning and connection. Whether it’s solving blindfolded, designing breathtaking mosaics or conducting workshops for students, he brings precision, showmanship and heart. His brand stands for: discipline, creativity, excellence and fun with purpose.

Join him on his journey—because every twist, turn and solved cube is part of a bigger story.
